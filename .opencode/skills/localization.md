# Skill: localization

## Scope
Dong bo noi dung `vi/en` trong app.

## Rules
- Them key moi vao ca `en` va `vi`.
- Dat ten key ro nghia, nhat quan theo module.
- Khong hard-code text trong widget neu co the localize.

## Checklist
- UI auth/shop/scan khong vo key.
- Toggle ngon ngu van giu trang thai qua app restart.
